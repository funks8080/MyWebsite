Who I am

Growing up near Wisconsin Dells and working at the tourist hotspots I have come across a wide range of folks that have helped change my outlook on life. Over the years I have changed my career outlook from Accountant, History Teacher, Creative Writer and finally settling on Computer Prgramming. This change to Computer Programming, came when I met my best friend at college who showed me the possibility of going further with my interest of video games where UW-Stout had just offered a new program based on Game Design. Taking this lead I found a new path, one that allowed me to use my skills and creativeness to put together the ideas that I had always thought for games.

I have always been interested in how games create their puzzels, the way a character levels, how the attributes of that character impact the game and how difficulty is judged. I have taken these interestes and applied them over the past few years to develope into the computer programmer I am today.
